PRIVACY POLICY Last Updated: [Date] Who We Are Overjoyed Ghost Studios (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is an independent game studio based in the United Kingdom. We create and distribute games and related online content. What Information We Collect • No Personal Data: We do not actively collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or address) when you visit our website or use our games. • Non-Personal Data: We may use basic analytics tools that automatically collect general, non-identifying information such as device type, operating system, and broad location (e.g., country). This information does not identify you personally. How We Use This Information • Analytics and Improvements: Any non-personal data collected helps us understand how our services are used, so we can maintain and improve our website and games. • No Marketing or Profiling: We do not use your data for targeted advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties. Your Rights If you reside in a region with specific data protection laws (such as the UK or EU), you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data we hold. Since we do not collect personal data, these requests typically do not apply. However, if you believe we have inadvertently collected your personal information (for example, if you contacted us directly and provided such details), please reach out, and we will delete it promptly. Security We take reasonable steps to protect any information we handle. However, no online platform is entirely risk-free, and you use our services at your own discretion. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with legal requirements.
